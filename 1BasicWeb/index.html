SIMPLICITY IS THE KEY

Simplicity could definitely be called the main characteristic of flat design and of the website you’re currently viewing. Here you can not only gain basic knowledge about flat design’s history, use and evolution but also about inspiration behind this website’s look.

Let’s start by having a quick glimpse at the mood board, which is supposed to be the essence of flat design’s characteristics. Flat design is all about shapes, vivid colors and contrast and despite its simplicity it continues to draw attention - that’s what this mood board is supposed to represent. It shows versatile nature of flat design - this style looks good on any kind of image, no matter if it’s a landscape, portrait or icons. Font choice also isn’t accidental - The Bold Font is a perfect blend of minimalistic and strong look.
