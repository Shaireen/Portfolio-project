about me

I'm a 19-year-old Polish girl interested in design, music and animals. I am currently living in Denmark and studying Multimedia Design and Communication at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. Even though Poland is my home country, my heart belongs to Denmark – that’s why I decided to move abroad and continue my education there.

It contains the most important projects from all of four themes, as well as short descriptions and, in some cases, the process of creating the final product. Each of the subpages is dedicated to one theme and includes screenshots, text and links to the original files or websites.
