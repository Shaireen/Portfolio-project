about me

I'm a 20-year-old Polish girl interested in design, music and animals. I am currently living in Denmark and studying Multimedia Design and Communication at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. Even though Poland is my home country, my heart belongs to Denmark – that’s why I decided to move abroad and continue my education there.

passion

I’m really passionate about web design and I find the process of creating a website extremely satisfying, especially when I finally manage to find a solution to all the issues. In my free time I like to draw, take photos, listen to music, learn Danish, cook and drink lots of coffee to keep me going. I also used to practice horse riding and had my own horse for a few years, it's probably still my biggest passion but unfortunately I had to give it up for now.
